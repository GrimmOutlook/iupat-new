Arundel Mills Mall

Arundel Mills is Maryland's top shopping, dining and entertainment destination, with over 200 stores including manufacturer and retail outlets, off-price retailers, and unique specialty stores. A festive food court, Medieval Times Dinner & Tournament, and the Cinemark Egyptian 24 are also part of the shopping adventure. The mall is located less than three miles away.

The Timbers at Troy

Voted one of the "Top 100 Golf Courses" in the Mid-Atlantic by Golfstyles magazine, and Rated 4-Stars by Golf Digest, the Timbers at Troy is your home course providing a breathtaking backdrop of the best countryside in Maryland. Undulating fairways, mature trees and meandering streams create a memorable golf experience that will challenge golfers at every level.

The Inner Harbor

From breath-taking panoramic views of the skyline from the Observation Level of the World Trade Center to the up-close and personal experiences of street performances happening spontaneously at the waterfront, Baltimore's Inner Harbor offers more to see and do than you might imagine. The Harbor is also home to a lot of Maryland's fine dining.
