Local Transportation

With a variety of places to visit while you are here, check out these ways to get around all of Maryland:

Amtrak- The BWI Airport station is approximately 11 miles south of Penn Station (BAL) in downtown Baltimore and 30 miles north of Union Station (WAS) in Washington, DC. A free bus transfer is available to BWI Airport terminals.

Maryland Transit Administration- The MTA offers several modes of transportation to get around all of Maryland, including the Metro Subway, Light Rail, MARC Train, and local buses.

Baltimore Water Taxi- Taking a boat tour is a memorable way to travel through and see Baltimore's attractions, and what makes it even better is that all IUPAT guests receive discounts for transportation.

Taxi Comapanies- Typically, a trip from BWI to the Inner Harbor is charged as a flat rate at $30.00. Most local places can be reached by taxi for $10 or less.
